# Reading Riverscapes

Welcome to my course portfolio for **Fluvial Geomorphology (CEWA 6150)** at Utah State University, taught by Joe Wheaton. This site is my virtual field notebook and assignment portfolio for the course.

## Site Sections

- **[Field Notebook](field-notebook/)** — observations and notes from riverscape visits
- **[Assignments](assignments/)** — my completed course assignments, by module
- **[Resources](resources/)** — references, tools, and links I found useful

## About the Course

The course follows *Geomorphic Analysis of River Systems: An Approach to Reading the Landscape* (Fryirs & Brierley), working from catchment-scale controls down through hydraulics, sediment transport, channel forms, floodplains, river diversity, behavior, and evolution — culminating in a final project reading contrasting river styles in a watershed of my choosing.

Course website: [Reading Riverscapes](https://riverscapes.github.io/Fluvial-Geomorphology/)
